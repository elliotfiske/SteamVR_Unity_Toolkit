*Salesforce Inbox Closes Huge $1.5M New Logo, Mohawk Industries #SIQWOW
<https://org62.my.salesforce.com/_ui/core/feeds/notification/TopicLandingPage?name=SIQWOW&ref=hash_mention&fromEmail=1&s1oid=00D000000000062&s1nid=000000000000000&s1uid=0053000000AVZyN&s1ext=0&emkind=chatterCommentNotification&emtm=1472774808656>*

Total Salesforce Inbox Deal Size: $117K for 1500 seats



*What does Mohawk do?*

Mohawk Industries is an American flooring manufacturer based in Georgia
producing floor covering products in North America and Europe. They
manufacture a variety of different flooring products from carpets and rugs
to tile and hardwood flooring.



*How did we find the opportunity and get buy in to add products to the
opportunity?*

Mohawk Industries’ previous implementation of their CRM failed due to lack
of adoption.  This meant they couldn't accurately forecast and managers
were left in the dark.  They were excited to move forward with UE licenses
and purchase. *Adele, the Core AE, heard they had issues with adoption and
the need to reduce time spent on administrative tasks, so she introduced
Salesforce Inbox as part of the overall solution.*



*What were their main challenges?*

*Mohawk Industries main challenge was that sales reps did not log activity
into their previous CRM.*  Because the CRM was not up to date, sales reps
weren't logging into the system and sales managers couldn’t get an
up-to-date view of where opportunities stood.  Mohawk Industries needed to
find a way to lessen the amount of time reps spent on manual data entry and
administrative tasks.



*Why was Salesforce Inbox the best product for them to solve these
challenges?*

*Salesforce Inbox was crucial* to a successful Sales Cloud rollout,
bridging the gap between email and CRM by:

   -

   *Reducing Manual Data Entry* for field reps by easily logging emails to
   Sales Cloud from your inbox.
   -

   *Preventing Reps from Missing Opportunities* with features built with
   data intelligence like read receipts and smart tasks.
   -

   *Increasing Pipeline Visibility* by creating records from your Inbox
   with a seamless Sales Cloud integration.



Fantastic win by Adele Sessions, AE, and Brittany Ivanco, SalesforceIQ
Co-Prime!
